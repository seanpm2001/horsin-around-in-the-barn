AirB&B description

Newly built barn with cozy "tack room". Room for you and your horse. Living area separate from bathroom and shower. You can peek in and talk to your horse while in the powder room. Noble panel stalls (3 stalls). Enjoy the country but still only 3 miles from downtown Walla Walla. Enjoy the wineries close by. Feed the horses and the chicken's if you'd like. Views of the Blue Mountains can't be beat from your king size bed. We would love to welcome you or relax in private.

The space

Our country life includes 3 horses and 10 chickens. They are not in the barn so you won't hear them. You may feed them if you like!

Guest access

You may walk around the house and enjoy the grass. We ask if you have a pet that you pick up after it. Please enter the horse pasture when we are present. You may visit the horses in the stables.

Interaction with guests

We can be attentive or leave you alone. We live in the house next to the barn.. please call my cell should you need us. You can email prior to arrival. You can come to the door and ring our bell. We'll be happy to help!
